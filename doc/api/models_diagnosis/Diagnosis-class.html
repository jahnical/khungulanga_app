Constructs a new instance of the Diagnosis class. id is the ID of the diagnosis. imageUrl is the image URL of the diagnosis. bodyPart is the body part of the diagnosis. itchy is whether the diagnosis is itchy or not. date is the date of the diagnosis. predictions is the predictions of the diagnosis. patient is the patient of the diagnosis. approved is whether the diagnosis is approved or not. action is the action of the diagnosis. dermatologist is the dermatologist of the diagnosis. extraDermInfo is the extra dermatologist information of the diagnosis.
